Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized sweet potatoes, baked and mashed
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Bake Sweet Potatoes: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Bake the sweet potatoes until tender. Once cooled, peel and mash them.
  2. Prepare Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed sweet potatoes, flour, Parmesan cheese, salt, nutmeg, and beaten egg. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead and Shape: Turn the dough out onto a floured surface. Knead it gently, adding more flour if needed to prevent sticking. Divide the dough into smaller portions and roll each into a long rope.
  4. Cut into Gnocchi: Cut the ropes into bite-sized pieces. Optionally, use a fork to create ridges on the gnocchi.
  5. Cook Gnocchi: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Drop the gnocchi into the boiling water and cook until they float to the surface, indicating they are done.
  6. Optional Sauté: For added flavor, sauté the cooked gnocchi in a pan with butter or olive oil until they develop a golden crust.
  7. Serve: Plate the sweet potato gnocchi and serve with your favorite sauce, such as brown butter and sage or a light tomato sauce. Garnish with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
